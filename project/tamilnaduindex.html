Ooty
Ooty, nestled in the Nilgiri Hills of southern India, is a picturesque hill station renowned for its lush green landscapes, cool climate, and serene lakes. Visitors flock to Ooty to experience its charming colonial architecture, tea plantations, and the scenic Nilgiri Mountain Railway, offering breathtaking views of the surrounding valleys.

Kodaikanal
Kodaikanal, nestled in the Palani Hills of Tamil Nadu, is a serene hill station renowned for its picturesque landscapes, cool climate, and tranquil lakes, making it a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Rameswaram
Rameswaram, located in Tamil Nadu, India, is renowned for its sacred Ramanathaswamy Temple, one of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites. It is famous for its religious significance as well as its pristine beaches.
