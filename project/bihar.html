Bodh gaya
Bodh Gaya, a sacred site in India, is where Gautama Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi tree, making it a pilgrimage destination for Buddhists worldwide. It's characterized by serene tranquility and spiritual significance, attracting seekers of inner peace and enlightenment.

Nalanda
Nalanda was a renowned ancient center of learning in India, flourishing from the 5th to 12th centuries CE. It attracted scholars and students from across the world, housing vast libraries and fostering intellectual exchange.
