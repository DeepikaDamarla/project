Rudrasagar Lake
Rudrasagar Lake, located in Tripura, India, is a serene water body surrounded by lush greenery, offering tranquil boat rides amidst picturesque scenery. It is famed for its floating palace, Neermahal, adding a touch of royal charm to its beauty.
